Visualization 3 Prototype

Visualization 3

Interpretation

This visualization tracks frequency of each type of reported crime and the particular police district the report was associated with during Dec 2018.

Discussion

The purpose of this visualization was to see if a particular region in San Francisco was either more crime heavy or had an unusual amount of a type of crime. However, without general population data, it is impossible to make such a conclusion from this heatmap.

Credit

A most of the bar chart generation code comes from Sophie Engle's blockbuilder example here: https://blockbuilder.org/sjengle/1e23258249638a508426470a48ff2924.
The data is from here: https://data.sfgov.org/Public-Safety/Police-Department-Incident-Reports-2018-to-Present/wg3w-h783
