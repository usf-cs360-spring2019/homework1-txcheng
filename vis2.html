Visualization 2

Interpretation

This visualization tracks the number of crimes committed on each hour of a day over the course of Dec 2018.

Discussion

The purpose of this visualization was to see if there were any trends in crime rate when looking at the time of day. As you can see, there seems to be more crimes committed from noon to midnight.

Credit

Most of the bar chart generation code comes from Sophie Engle's blockbuilder example here: https://blockbuilder.org/sjengle/1e23258249638a508426470a48ff2924.
The data is from here: https://data.sfgov.org/Public-Safety/Police-Department-Incident-Reports-2018-to-Present/wg3w-h783
